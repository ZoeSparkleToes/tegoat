Peagle - Scotland



The peagle is a very unique animal native to Scotland and some of them are spotted flying over to Sweden.
They have an incredible history and they are predators to many insects and pests. Scotlandian people have
started to keep them as pets in 1745 when a Scotland king found one and had one as a pet. Since then, many households
in Scotland have had Peagles as pets. Their life expantcay is from 30 - 40 years long



Chimpanda - North Korea



The Chimpanda is very exotic creature for the royalty of North Korea. They are very protected and nearly extinct.
North Korea has royal habitats for many families of Chimpandas. Chimpandas are very social animals,
some scientists even think that they have made their own language, presumably called "Chimpandeeze"



Chickuahua - Mexico



The Chickuahua is a very social animal, like the Chimpanda, but the Chickuahua are known for their mating and
romanticness. They are also very elegant, and many can dance like or better than humans. They also have many hobbies, such as
swimming, digging, and even running. They can also very easily glide for a few meters and are sometimes very ferocious.



Howls-Russia



In the wilds of Russia, there exists a mystical creature—the Rusowl Wolf, known locally as the Howls.
With a coat as pure as the snow it treads upon, this majestic being be ars the face of an owl, complete with wise, amber eyes.
Legends speak of the Howls as a guardian spirit of the forests, blending the intelligence of the owl with the loyalty of the wolf, silently watching over the land under the moon's glow.

Tigerhawk-Siberia



In the remote wilderness of Siberia, a formidable and awe-inspiring creature roams—the Tiger Hawks.
These majestic beings are a striking fusion of the strength and grace of a tiger with the keen eyesight and aerial prowess of hawks.
With their sleek, striped bodies and powerful wings spanning great distances, they command the skies and the earth below.
Legends whisper of their ability to hunt with unmatched precision, swooping down from the skies to capture prey with the ferocity of a tiger and the precision of a hawk.
Yet, despite their formidable nature, the Tiger Hawks are also revered as guardians of the Siberian wilderness, their presence a symbol of the untamed beauty and raw power of the region.

Elecat-Kenya


In the vast plains of Kenya, amidst the towering acacia trees and golden grasslands, roams a creature both majestic and enigmatic—the Elecat.
This unique being is a wondrous blend of the grandeur of an elephant and the cunning charm of a cat.

With its massive frame and tusks gleaming in the African sun, it commands respect and admiration.
Yet, it is the Elecat's face, adorned with whiskers and feline features, that truly sets it apart.
Legends whisper of its agility and stealth, as it prowls through the savannah with the grace of a cat, yet carries the strength of an elephant.
Revered by local tribes as a symbol of wisdom and cunning, the Elecat is a testament to the diverse and magical wonders of the Kenyan wilderness.
